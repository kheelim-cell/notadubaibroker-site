The Problem

Brokers Are Paid to Sell,
Not to Advise

Most Dubai property agents are commission-driven. That means their advice is rarely truly impartial — and your investment decisions shouldn't rely on it.

Overly Optimistic Projections

Brokers routinely present best-case rental yields and capital appreciation figures that don't hold up under independent scrutiny.

Pressure-Selling Tactics

Artificial urgency and fear-of-missing-out language push buyers into rushed decisions on high-value, long-term commitments.

First-Time Buyer Vulnerability

Investors new to Dubai lack the frameworks to independently assess deals — leaving them exposed to poor value purchases.
